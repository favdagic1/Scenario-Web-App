INT. STARSHIP BRIDGE - NIGHT

Alarms BLARE. Red lights flash across the control panels. The crew scrambles to their stations.

CAPTAIN KIRA bursts through the door, her expression grim.

KIRA

Report! What's our status?

CHEN

Hull breach on deck seven. We're losing pressure fast.

KIRA

Seal it off. Now!

(to communications officer)

Get me engineering.

INT. ENGINE ROOM - NIGHT

Sparks fly from damaged control panels. Steam hisses from broken pipes.

MARCUS

We can't hold this together much longer, Captain!

KIRA

You have to, Marcus. We need five more minutes.

MARCUS

I'll do what I can.

EXT. ASTEROID FIELD - DAY

The starship weaves through massive floating rocks, narrowly avoiding collision.

CHEN

We're clear of the field!

KIRA

Take us home, Chen. Take us home.
